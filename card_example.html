PRESETS

Cardio Ability

Determined by what option you choose, your choice will update the algorithim to accomodate accordingly. For example, if you are "rarely active," the algorithim will not advise you to take up a delivery that exceeds 5 kilometers.

Here is the following definitions:

Rarely Active --- Can cycle for about 5km
Sometimes Active --- Can cyle for about 10km
Active --- Can cycle for about 20km
Very Active --- Can cycle for about 40km

Available Time

Determined by how much time you have, a countdown timer will appear to help you take care of your time, and in extreme cases of a delivery that takes 25% of your time without a large payout, it will advise you to not take up the delivery.

Area Lock

Determined by if it is on or not, Area Lock automatically advises you to not accept a delivery if the delivery is more than 10 kilometers away.

DELIVERY SPECIFIC INFORMATION

Estimated Delivery Time

Here, you input the estimated time of the delivery, supplied to you by the delivery app. Be sure to input the time in minutes. For example, (although unreleastic) say Uber says it will take one hour and fifteen minutes, input 75 minutes.

Estimated Delivery Earnings

Here, you input the estimated earnings of the delivery, supplied to you by the delivery app. Be sure to input the earnings in the $ format: 00.00. Later on, we will add compatibility for other currencies.

Estimated Delivery Distance

Here, you input the estimated distance of the delivery, supplied to you by the delivery app. Be sure to input the distance in meters. Later on, we will add compatibility for other currencies.
